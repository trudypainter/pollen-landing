About Pollen.so
Pollen
Pollen is a research studio fascinated by early computing ideas that saw the computer as a tool for the technologist, creator, and artist. We are developing novel web platforms to foster collaborative environments across culture and technology.
What are we building?
Currently, we’re rethinking information storytelling and large scale community contribution.
Our first product is Pollen.so → a contribution space for web groups, online audiences, and DAOs.
